Support:
Options for your support.
1)Make a Ticket: You can create a ticket in-game, press System Menu then Petitions after that choose your problem type through the combo box and type your problem with as much details you can.
2)Forum Topic: Go and register if not yet in our forums www.l2server.com/forum and go to General Boards->Report & Support then create a topic by clicking "New Topic" use as subject your problem title and then write your problem with as much details.
3)Send an Email or PM: You can send a pm at Optimus on the Forum or send an email at support@l2server.com again with as much details you can. I suggest pm than email because it is checked more frequently.
